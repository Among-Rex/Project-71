Came first in the WACSSA Interschool Athletics Carnival

Last week Carey Harrisdale Primary students from Years 3-6 competed in the WACSSA Interschool Athletics Carnival – Division 1. After 2 days of competition including track and field events, our students came away the winners, taking out first place!

  • Year 6 Camp

    Our Harrisdale Year 6 students had a fantastic camp in Busselton; full of adventure, friendship and making amazing memories! Some highlights were the night walk along the Busselton Jetty, zip-lining through the trees, beach games, rafting, visiting Calgardup Cave, plus a fun and interactive morning learning about Aboriginal culture. The last night of camp was a Camp Concert ‘Jungle Theme’. There were an array of jungle animals, safari hunters and all things jungle represented in the costumes. The students delighted everyone with a selection of songs, jokes, dances and riddles before a dance party to end the night! It was a fabulous week and wonderful to see the students stepping out of their comfort zones and developing their teamwork skills, plus having a lot of fun along the way!

  • Secondary School 2021

    School closures If there was to be a confirmed COVID-19 case at Carey, the decision to close the college would be on the advice of the WA Health Department’s Chief Health Officer. The Chief Health Officer will, if there has been a positive COVID-19 test result, close the school temporarily while assessment and tracing of exposure is conducted. A thorough school clean will then be carried out to make the school safe for the return of students and staff. In this situation, all Carey families will be immediately contacted by the College and provided with the relevant information. What the School is currently doing to minimise the risk of infection Considering the recent government COVID-19 announcement, banning large group gatherings and emphasising the need for social distancing, we have now made the difficult decision to postpone or cancel several additional events planned for Term 1 and Term 2.
